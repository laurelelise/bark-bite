At Bark & Bite we like everything to be made from scratch from thoughtfully sourced ingredients. We think hot sauce, jams, and pickles should stand on their own and not just play backup to a good meal.

Over the past few years we have taken a lot of care in developing our hot sauce and condiments into products that we love. Our goal with Bark & Bite has been to create a products made from the best ingredients, with some serious heat and flavor, that you can keep eating. We like our condiments hot, yet approachable, like all of the best things in life.

We hope you will enjoy our products as much as we do. Hand-crafted with all organic ingredients, that are cultured through a raw, fermented process, we know we've made something that not only tastes good, but is good for you as well.

If you're like us, everything is better with a little hot sauce (or kraut, or jam). Be sure to show us how you like to use Bark & Bite and tag @barkbike on instagram because we're always looking for inventive ways to make life taste better.
--Eddie G.
